The Crooked Knife

While exploring the history of the area of the original location, we found that was considered at one time, and still by some, to be the northern boundary of Gramercy. the name, “Gramercy", is an Anglicization of "CROMESSIE", itself DERIVED FROM THE DUTCH, KROM MESJE, OR "LITTLE CROOKED KNIFE" the name of a small brook or stream meandering like a 'CROOKED LITTLE KNIFE’ or 'CROM MESSIE.' ... the descision was made to drop the 'little'... and 'THE CROOKED KNIFE" was born!!

About the Crooked Knife

The Butcher's Daughter

The Butcher’s Daughter is a plant-based restaurant, cafe, juice bar and “vegetable slaughterhouse." Our four locations serve breakfast, lunch, dinner and weekend brunch. We take pride in our daily-changing menu, which is 100% vegetarian, and mostly vegan and gluten-free.

About the Butcher's Daughter

NoMo Kitchen

NoMo Kitchen offers a rustic American menu with Mediterranean influences helmed by Chef Jesse Olson. Serving breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a truly unique weekend brunch (if you’ve attend, you know what we’re talking about), our ever-changing menus showcase farm-to-fork seasonal cuisine.
