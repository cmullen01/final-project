NoMo lives at the intersection of Nostalgic and Modern: 9 Crosby Street. During the 50s, 60s, and 70s, SoHo became synonymous with fashion, design, art, and culture —serving as an inspirational proving ground for some of the world’s most wayward trendsetters. Today, SoHo is a hotbed of creative up-and-comers, in addition to housing established brands, well-known artists, and cultural icons. NoMo blends the nostalgic with the modern for a unique, yet authentic, SoHo experience.

Bar/Lounge/Restaurant | 24-Hour Front Desk | SoHo Perks | Dry Cleaning Service | Valet Parking Concierge | Free Newspaper | 24/7 Fitness Center & Gym

NoMo Kitchen offers a rustic American menu with Mediterranean influences helmed by Chef Jesse Olson. Serving breakfast, lunch, dinner, and a truly unique weekend brunch (if you’ve attend, you know what we’re talking about), our ever-changing menus showcase farm-to-fork seasonal cuisine.

What’s cuisine without cocktails? We also offer and expertly curated, handcrafted, seasonal cocktail program overseen by master mixologist Xavier Herit.
